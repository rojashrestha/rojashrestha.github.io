About Me

Hello there! I'm Roja Shrestha

Hi, I’m Roja Shrestha. I’m learning, discovering, and becoming. I love exploring creativity through technology, journaling, and storytelling. I’m currently pursuing Computer Engineering at Purbanchal University. Everything you see here reflects my journey, honest, imperfect, and always evolving.

I haven’t quite figured out what I truly love as a Computer Science student, but being creative feels like an essential part of me. For now, I simply want to be a creative person in the room, someone who keeps learning the smallest artistic details that make things beautiful.

Experience

Creative Lead, ACIT (2022 to 2025)

ACIT is a student-run IT committee at my college, and it has always been more than just a club; it is an emotion for me. I contributed to the creative and technical activities of the ACIT club through design, coordination, event organization, and leadership.

Creative Affair, TEDxKamalpokhari (January 2024 to June 2024)

TEDxKamalpokhari is an independently organized event that brings together diverse voices to share and spread inspiring ideas. As part of the creative team, I helped enhance and amplify its visual and digital presence, strengthening its creative content and engagement online.
