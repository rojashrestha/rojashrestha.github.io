About Me

Hello there! I'm Roja Shrestha

Hi, I’m Roja Shrestha. I’m learning, discovering, and becoming. I love exploring creativity through technology, journaling, and storytelling. I’m currently pursuing Computer Engineering at Purbanchal University. Everything you see here reflects my journey honest, imperfect, and always evolving.

I haven’t quite figured out what I truly love as a Computer Science student, but being creative feels like an essential part of me. For now, I want to be the creative person in the room someone who keeps learning the small artistic details that make things beautiful.
